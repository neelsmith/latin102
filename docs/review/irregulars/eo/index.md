# *Eo* at a glance

Principal parts:

*eo, ire, ii* or *iui, -*

(Active) infinitives:

present: *ire*, perfect: *isse*



Imperfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| ibat | ibant |

Perfect indicative

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| iit, iuit | ierunt, iuerunt |


Imperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| iret | irent |


Pluperfect subjunctive

| Third singular | Third plural |
| --- | --- |
| isset | issent |
