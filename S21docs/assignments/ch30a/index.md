## Glosses and notes

*duabus* irregular form of *duo, duae, ,duo*, "two": fem.abl.pl.

*unde* a subordinating conjunction, "from where" ("whence" in Victorian lexicon speak!)


## Syntactic analysis

> Infans cum esset, 

dracones duos duabus manibus necauit, 

> quos Iuno miserat, 

>> unde primigenius est dictus
