# Week 1 vocabulary


## Common prepositions

- ab or a+ abl: away from
- ad + acc: towards
- apud + acc: at, with, by, near
- cum + abl: with
- de + abl: from, down from
- ex + abl: out of
- in + abl: in
- in + acc: into
- inter + acc: between, among
- ob + acc: on account of
- per + acc: through
- post + acc: after
- pro + abl: in front of, on behalf of
- propter + acc: on account of
- super + acc: above, on top of
